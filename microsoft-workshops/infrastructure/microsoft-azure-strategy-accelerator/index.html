What to expect from the Microsoft Azure Strategy Accelerator

Whether you are exploring Azure for the first time or looking to deepen your strategic understanding, this workshop promises to deliver valuable takeaways tailored to your leadership role. Here’s what you can expect to take away from this transformative session*:

 

How to plan your cloud adoption strategy 

Understanding your motivations for a cloud data centre or cloud native approach is crucial as it helps you align with your business goals. This workshop will help you assess your readiness, understand how to review your cloud strategy for continuous improvement, and measure business outcomes from your cloud investments.  

Building an Azure Landing Zone 

An Azure Landing Zone is key if you want to adopt and maintain best practices to secure your investments. We’ll share how by embedding secure by design or zero trust principles, you can build an agile foundation that evolves to meet new business challenges.  

Optimising your Azure environment for FinOps 

We’ll share the criticality of resource organisation and tagging, demonstrate how to implement near real-time usage and billing insights, and show you how to assess your FinOps maturity. Emphasis will be placed on identifying quick wins and discussion on the value of outlier costs versus highest costs and moving from automation to broader transformation to maximise efficiency and cost-effectiveness. 

Enhance your Azure cloud architecture 

In this workshop, we’ll help you understand how and where to innovate and why implementing cloud governance and management is essential. We’ll delve into the necessity of conducting regular architecture reviews to adapt to evolving business plans and cloud services, which ultimately save you money and prevent potential issues. Additionally, the workshop will clarify the critical distinctions between availability and recoverability, and how these aspects significantly influence your cloud model and your business continuity strategy.    

Securing your Azure environment

You'll gain a comprehensive understanding of the risks to your organisation and learn how to secure against them effectively. The session will emphasise the importance of implementing secure cloud baselines, highlighting why this is paramount for any cloud strategy. Additionally, we'll guide you along ensuring you have the appropriate cloud security technologies in place, equipping you with the knowledge to protect your cloud environments robustly. 

* The discussion themes are examples of potential workshop topics. The actual content will be tailored to address your specific business needs and challenges. Our goal is to ensure that the workshop is customer-focused, delivering tangible business value that can drive your organisation's digital transformation and enhance your competitive edge.
