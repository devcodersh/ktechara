Microsoft Dynamics 365 & Business Applications

Business applications are designed to simplify and automate essential organisational processes. They include solutions for Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Customer Relationship Management (CRM).

ERP platforms bring together core operations, such as finance, supply chain, projects and manufacturing, into a single system, improving efficiency and enabling smarter decision-making. CRM systems help businesses manage customer interactions, optimise sales workflows, strengthen relationships, and drive revenue growth.

Microsoft Dynamics 365 is a cloud-based suite that combines ERP and CRM capabilities in one integrated platform. It works seamlessly with Microsoft 365, Azure, Power Platform and Copilot, giving organisations the tools to connect data, processes, and people for greater agility and insight.

What are the benefits of business applications

Automate routine tasks, modernise workflows, boost productivity, and optimise supply chain management with AI-driven ERP and CRM solutions. With Microsoft Dynamics 365 seamlessly integrated with Microsoft 365, Azure, and Copilot, you can manage sales, marketing, service, finance, and supply chain operations from a single, adaptable platform, built to scale as your business grows. Copilot for Dynamics 365 uses agentic AI capabilities to streamline business operations, offering chat-based insights for sales opportunities and leads, email content generation, and seamless integration with Outlook and Teams for better data management.

Operate smarter

Cut down on repetitive, manual tasks, save time, and reduce errors with intelligent automation that streamlines workflows.

Accelerate sales

Equip your sales teams with tools for automated lead management, predictive pipeline analytics, and actionable insights to close deals faster.

Optimise supply chains

Gain complete visibility across purchasing, inventory, and warehouse operations to improve stock control and keep your supply chain running efficiently.

Frequently asked questions

What’s the difference between ERP, CRM, and other business applications?

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems integrate core business processes such as finance, supply chain, and operations into a single platform for greater efficiency. CRM (Customer Relationship Management) focuses on managing customer interactions, sales, and service to strengthen relationships and drive growth.

Other business applications include tools for project management, marketing automation, and business intelligence. Each serves a unique purpose, but together they can create a unified, connected business ecosystem.

How do I choose the right business application for my organisation?

Start by aligning your choice with your business goals and operational needs. Key factors to consider include integration with existing systems, ease of use, scalability, vendor support, and total cost of ownership. 

Our solutions consultants can work with you to assess, plan, and deliver a secure, cost-effective CRM or ERP deployment tailored to your organisation. 

What is Microsoft Dynamics 365?

Microsoft Dynamics 365 is a suite of ERP and CRM applications designed to help organisations manage essential functions like finance, operations, sales, customer service, and marketing. 

The platform includes solutions such as Dynamics 365 Sales (CRM), Dynamics 365 Business Central (ERP), and Dynamics 365 Field Service. It integrates seamlessly with Microsoft 365 and Power Platform, enabling workflow automation, data insights, and improved collaboration across teams.

As a Microsoft Cloud Solutions Partner with a Business Applications designation, our certified Dynamics 365 and Power Platform experts design, configure, and implement solutions that streamline operations, enhance customer engagement, and support long-term growth.
