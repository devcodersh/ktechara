Frequently asked questions

What are managed IT services?

Managed IT services allow you to source the provision of maintenance, proactive management and optimisation of your IT systems from experienced service providers.

A managed service provider (MSP) delivers a comprehensive range of services and solutions including service desk, device and endpoint management, identity and access management, network management, cloud management and optimisation, cyber security, technical support and more.

Why partner with an MSP?

Partnering with a Managed Services Provider (MSP) gives you access to specialised expertise and advanced technologies without the cost and complexity of building those capabilities in-house.  

An MSP helps you reduce risk, improve resilience, and optimise costs by delivering proactive monitoring, cyber security, and round-the-clock support.

With predictable pricing models and scalable services, you can focus on strategic priorities while your MSP ensures your IT environment runs smoothly, securely, and efficiently. This partnership also helps you keep pace with rapid technological change and maintain compliance without overstretching internal resources.
