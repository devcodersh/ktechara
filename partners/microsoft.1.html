A WORLD-CLASS UK MICROSOFT PARTNER IN YOUR CORNER

Helping you achieve and inspire with Microsoft solutions every day

We’re one of the UK’s leading Azure, Dynamics 365 and Microsoft 365 partners. For you, this means being able to tap into expertise and thought leadership as we work together.

What a leading Microsoft partner gives you

Full value from your Microsoft investments

You want a partner who treats your satisfaction as their first priority. As an award-winning Microsoft consultancy, our client successes are a tribute to our commitment to your needs. A full Solutions Partner for Microsoft Cloud, our experience lets us deliver for our clients every time. With vast experience in the Microsoft ecosystem, we help ambitious organisations take full advantage of Microsoft Azure, Security, Dynamics 365, Microsoft 365 and industry solutions to build toward long-term success.

Solutions Partner for Microsoft Cloud

Our breadth of strengths and specialisations

As one of the most awarded Microsoft partners in the UK and a leading voice in the global Microsoft ecosystem, KTechara holds Solutions Partner designations for Business Applications, Data and AI, Digital and App Innovation, Modern Work, Infrastructure and Security.

Achieving all of these marks us out as a Solutions Partner for Microsoft Cloud – the highest possible recognition. 

This elite level of acknowledgement demonstrates our capabilities and experience across a wide range of technical needs, with the ability to deliver client success aligned to the Microsoft cloud.

We also hold ten specialisations under five of our Solutions Partner designations, placing us in the global elite of Microsoft partners. Our specialisations are:

  • Adoption and Change Management
  • AI Platform on Microsoft Azure
  • Azure Virtual Desktop
  • Cloud Security
  • Copilot
  • Identity and Access Management
  • Information Protection and Governance
  • Low Code Application Development
  • Modernise Endpoints
  • Small and Midsize Business Management
  • Threat Protection

Microsoft’s Partner of the Year for Education

Empowering educators to inspire learners

We’re delighted to be Microsoft’s 2023 Partner of the Year for Education. The global award recognises KTechara as having provided outstanding solutions and services, and having demonstrated innovation and excellence with Microsoft technology, for the education sector.

With more than 4,200 nominations submitted from around the world, Microsoft’s Partner of the Year awards recognise the very best organisations globally. 

It’s the second year in a row we have been recognised, following our Customer Experience award in 2022.

Unlocking possibility with Microsoft technology

Realise your full potential with the technology you use every day

Keeping up with the breadth, depth and fast-evolving functionality within Microsoft’s cloud technologies can often be a challenge that even the most dedicated of IT teams struggle with. 

That’s where we come in. We train and accredit our team to the highest levels and recruit the most capable talent (including four Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals). We have invested in keeping pace with Microsoft’s evolution – to deliver the benefits back to our clients. Because of this, we have built one of the market’s leading relationships with Microsoft – built on trust and their confidence in our ability to deliver.

We sit on Microsoft’s Partnership Advisory Councils, we work with their senior developers and product leads, helping to design future products and services; and we engage with their account and leadership teams to collectively meet the needs and demands of our clients.

Jon Jarvis

MVP CATEGORY:

Microsoft Intune (Security) and Windows 365 (Windows and Devices)

MVP AWARDS:

3 since 2023

Jon Jarvis is a Microsoft Field Technologist at KTechara UK, specialising in Modern Work, Security, Intune, and Windows 365. A Microsoft MVP in both Security and Windows 365, Jon works closely with organisations across the UK to shape digital workplace strategy, modernise endpoints, and unlock value from Microsoft 365 and cloud-native technologies.

With deep experience spanning advisory-led engagements, workshops, and enterprise-scale modernisation projects, Jon bridges technical depth and business outcomes—helping customers move from legacy approaches to secure, scalable, cloud-first models. He is also an active community contributor, regularly sharing practical insights through articles, webinars, and events to help demystify modern workplace and security transformation.

IN HIS OWN WORDS:

The MVP community is something that I have always looked to for guidance since the beginning of my IT career, if it was seeking out written guidance for implementations or general problem solving. A part of me always wanted to help the IT community be it through written articles, videos or speaking at conferences. Being a part of a community that always helped me is a privilege and I always hope that I can use my voice to help others.
