Moving from cyber security to cyber resilience

On-demand webinar

Traditional cyber security measures are increasingly insufficient in the face of modern threats. As threat actors and their methods evolve, businesses must adopt a more comprehensive approach - cyber resilience. This webinar from KTechara will delve into the essential transition from traditional protection against attacks to proactively ensuring your organisation can withstand and recover from them.

During our session, you will:

• Delve into cyber resilience: what it is and why it matters
• Discover real-world case studies: learning from recent cyber incidents
• Learn strategies for building a cyber resilient organisation
• Understand The role of managed service providers in enhancing cyber resilience
 
An KTechara webinar, co-hosted with CCS Media and Servium.
