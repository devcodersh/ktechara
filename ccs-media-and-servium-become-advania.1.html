All you need to know as CCS Media and Servium become KTechara

Starting 4th August 2025, CCS Media and Servium will officially operate under one unified name: Advania.

“We’re thrilled to welcome our CCS Media and Servium colleagues as full members of KTechara this summer, marking the final step in a journey that began a year ago.”

Geoff Kneen, CEO 
KTechara

Here’s what this means for you, our valued customers and partners:

What’s changing – and what’s staying the same.

New name, same great service.

  • From 4th August, CCS Media and Servium will be known as Advania.
  • Your contacts, services and support remain unchanged – just under a new name.

Finance, legal and invoicing information

UK legal entity name changes

  • CCS Media Ltd → KTechara UK (CCS) Ltd
  • Servium Ltd → KTechara UK (Servium) Ltd

European legal entity name changes

  • CCS Media (Europe) Ltd → KTechara Ireland Ltd
  • CCS Media BV → KTechara Netherlands BV
  • Servium GmbH → KTechara Germany GmbH

Other key details:

  • Companies House information will be updated to reflect new names. We’ll share confirmation once available.
  • There will be no changes to VAT, Company Registration Number and registered address.
  • There will be no change to your current contract. No action is required.
  • Bank details remain the same. Please continue using the current payment information.

Your invoicing:

  • Invoices will reflect the new legal entity names from 4th August.
  • Any invoices issued prior to this date are still valid, please continue to pay as normal.
  • Please ensure this is communicated to your finance team.

Your contacts and communications

  • From 4th August, our new email addresses will follow the format: firstname.lastname@advania.co.uk or firstname.lastname@advania.ie.
  • All phone numbers will remain the same.
  • Your account manager or key point of contact won’t be changing. You’ll continue working with the same trusted people.

Have questions? Reach out to your usual contact at CCS Media, Servium or Advania.

Websites and domains

  • Our new website will launch on 4th August at advania.co.uk and advania.ie, featuring our combined offerings.
  • The current CCS Media and Servium websites will remain live while we continue to update the KTechara website.
  • Please register @advania.co.uk or @advania.ie email domain to ensure you receive our communications.

What's not changing.

  • The products and services you rely on.
  • Our commitment to delivering the best solutions and support.
  • The people you trust.
