Cloudtango names Content+Cloud as a top managed service provider

Written by
  • Advania UK
Posted On
  • 7 Mar 2023
Duration of read
2  min
Share Article
Related Topics

Content+Cloud is proud to be named in Cloundtango’s top 100 global managed service providers (MSPs) for enterprise. 

Cloudtango has released the ninth edition of its global 100 list, highlighting MSPs that provide technical excellence, innovation and customer satisfaction. This year, the focus was on cyber security, support, infrastructure and cloud services, but business growth, customer satisfaction and service offerings also came into consideration.  

How a managed service provider can help you

The Global 100 Enterprise list highlights MSPs that play a key role in helping organisations drive innovation. Being named as one of the top MSPs demonstrates Content+Cloud’s excellent track record delivering innovative IT services while exceeding expectations.  

Cloudtango Managing Director Jordi Vilanova said: “Through the selection process, we identify the top performing Managed Service Providers during the year and we selected Content+Cloud based on their overall performance and continual service improvements.” 

Choosing the right managed service provider

MSPs play a vital role in ensuring organisations can operate successfully, especially when they are challenged with optimising complex IT services without straining their budgets. Cloudtango’s annual list helps organisations to know which MSPs can bring them the most value in the enterprise market. 

The Cloudtango awards have become a staple and trusted industry reference, offering independent, unbiased approach and in-depth analysis.  

We’re proud to be carrying on the track record of our predecessor company IT Lab, which consistently ranked in Cloudtango’s lists year on year, including one of the world’s top 500 MSPs for six consecutive years, one of the UK’s top 50 MSPs in 2020 and No.1 MSP in the world in 2019.  

Celebrating Content+Cloud’s awards

Content+Cloud is proud to add this award in Cloudtango’s Global 100 Enterprise MSP list to our growing list of accolades. Most recently, we’ve been celebrating our Microsoft Catalyst Partner status, not to mention the award of the sixth Microsoft Solutions Partner designation for Business Applications, making us a Solutions Partner for Microsoft Cloud – one of just four in the UK.   

About Cloudtango

Cloudtango’s mission is to help businesses connect with MSPs. Technology helps drive innovation and Cloudtango believes that MSPs play a vital role in turning IT into a distinct advantage. Cloudtango provides technology leaders with information and analysis to help them navigate their cloud journey and guide them to the right MSP to succeed.
